About IT Committee

An IT committee, also known as an Information Technology committee, is a group within an organization responsible for overseeing and guiding the strategic direction of technology initiatives. These committees are typically composed of key stakeholders from various departments within the organization, including IT professionals, executives, department heads, and sometimes representatives from external partners or vendors. The primary purpose of an IT committee is to ensure that technology investments align with the organization's goals and objectives while effectively managing risks and resources.
